Our story

A studio that feels like your best friend's house.

Bloom House started with one chair, one mirror, and a simple idea: beauty appointments should be the easiest hour of your week. Today we're a full team of stylists, nail artists, and estheticians — but the house rule hasn't changed. You walk in, exhale, and let us take it from here.

  • Free consultations before every color or first visit
  • Hospital-grade sanitation on every tool, every time
  • Cruelty-free product lines across hair, nails, and skin
  • Walk-ins welcome when the chairs are free
